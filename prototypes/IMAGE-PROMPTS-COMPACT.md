## Key Rules
- ✅ Always say "top-down"
- ✅ Always include "dark background"
- ✅ Use hex codes if tool supports
- ❌ Never "realistic" or "3D render"
- ❌ Never "side view"

---

## Sizes Needed
- **Sprite:** 1024x1024px (main character)
- **Icon:** 128x128px (UI thumbnail)
- **Closeup:** 512x512px (head detail)
